The N-Queens Sketchpad



This is a tool for students learning backtracking and working on the N-Queens problem. This sketchpad allows users to find solutions to an NxN board by hand to better understand the nature of the problem and the process of solving it.

Idea by Mr. Holmes. Coded by Haley Zeng (Stuy '17)
